What is this product?

Have you ever wondered if there was an app that could tell you anything about youself and predict your future based on your current habits? Give you projections of your life. Give you predictions of outcomes based on your entries. The applications of this idea are as limitless as our imagination and willingness to enter information. Our phycial condition, our mental state, our nutrition, our daily habits, our financial inputs/outputs. This idea can keep track of everything and make us better people. This app aims to be someones personal journal that isn't just an input but an output.
